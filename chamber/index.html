Welcome to the Auckland Business Chamber, an integral part of New Zealand's largest business network organization and a proud member of the New Zealand Chambers of Commerce network. At the heart of our mission lies a dedication to fostering vibrant and thriving communities by supporting businesses and advocating for the best conditions in which they can thrive.

From the 2degrees Auckland Business Awards to discussions with local Government, find and attend events that will support you in business.

Becoming a Chamber member is just good business! Whether you are a start-up, a connector, an innovator, a marketer, a networker, or an entrepreneur - the Chamber is the perfect place for you.
